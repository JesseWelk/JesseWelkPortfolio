Ecoture

The creation of Ecoture came from the idea of how fast fashion is one of the largest contributors to global warming. So is there a way to see what impact it might have on our carbon footprint?

Design

The project began with brainstorming of what the essence of the web app should be about, our planet. Through the use of Figma early designs for the wireframe of the app was created. from there creating prototypes of the final app was the main priority.

Front End

After the final stage of app prototyping it came time to translate it into code. There was a heavy use of React in the project to accomplish the look and function of the webapp.

User Testing

Once the app had reached a certain level of functionality it came time for user testing to determine if the flow of the app. The testing involved five individuals that went through the app and would be questioned at each point of the test about the apps functionality, design, and accessibility. Then the tester would be questioned on their overall experiance and if there was any changes they would make. A majority of testers had a positive experiance using that app which proved it was in it's final stages
